MAGGIE RECEPIE

chalo maggie banaye

INGRIDIENTS

  • Magii
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add magii to boiling water.
    1. Add salt to taste.
    2. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add sauce to a pan
  4. Mix cooked magii with sauce.
  5. Enjoy!
